The Problem

Booking a GP appointment should not feel like a barrier

The NHS online booking system serves over 30 million users, yet consistently scores low on accessibility and usability benchmarks. For older adults and people with health anxiety, friction in this process has real consequences ‚Äî missed appointments, increased A&E load, and eroded trust in digital services.
